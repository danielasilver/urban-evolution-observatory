Urban Evolution Observatory

Evidence about how cities persist, recombine, and change.

The Observatory publishes public reports from a larger research program on urban evolution. Each report starts with a claim, shows what the data can and cannot see, and explains how the evidence changes the theory.

How To Read This Site

The reports treat uncertainty as a finding. A result may support a claim, weaken it, split it into narrower claims, or show that a better dataset is needed. Public tables and methods notes are included so readers can inspect the basis for each interpretation.
